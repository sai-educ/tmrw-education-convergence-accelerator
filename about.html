About the Accelerator

The GEMS Convergence Accelerator is a global initiative within tmrw Education that connects evidence, innovation, and practice to advance responsible AI in K–12 learning.

Purpose

To establish GEMS as the global reference point for how AI improves schooling, focusing on evidence, ethics, and scalable impact across one of the world’s largest private school networks.

Principles

  • Evidence-based and practice-validated
  • Educator-led and human-centered
  • Ethical, safe, and transparent
  • Collaborative and open by design
  • Globally inclusive and data-informed

Three Phases

  • Phase 1: Landscape scan, pilot design, and initial implementations.
  • Phase 2: Evidence generation, capacity building, and ethical infrastructure.
  • Phase 3: Scaling proven models and convening global leadership events.
