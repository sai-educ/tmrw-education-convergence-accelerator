About the Convergence Accelerator

We exist to connect research and classroom practice, accelerating ethical, effective AI for K–12 learners and teachers worldwide.

Mission

To catalyze a global community that designs, tests, and scales AI-powered learning systems that improve equity, personalization, and creativity—grounded in evidence and classroom realities.

Principles

  • Human-centered and educator-led
  • Evidence-informed and practice-validated
  • Ethical, safe, and transparent by design
  • Open sharing and reproducibility
  • Global inclusion and accessibility

What “Convergence” Means

Convergence integrates multiple disciplines—learning sciences, computer science, design, ethics, and policy—to produce solutions that no single field could achieve alone.

Research ↔ Practice

Rapid cycles of piloting in classrooms ensure AI tools are useful, usable, and responsible.

Community

Our Microsoft Teams hub hosts working groups, clinics, office hours, and events.

Impact

We publish artifacts—playbooks, datasets, and case studies—to amplify what works.
