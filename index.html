Our Mission

Generate Evidence

Run pilots in GEMS schools to study how AI enhances teaching, learning, and operational efficiency.

Build Capacity

Develop the GEMS AI Teacher Academy to strengthen professional learning and responsible AI use.

Lead Globally

Establish GEMS as the global reference point for ethical and evidence-based AI in education.

Three-Phase Roadmap

Phase 1: Discovery & Pilots

Launch pilot projects with leading AI providers across six to eight GEMS schools, guided by measurable outcomes.

Phase 2: Evidence & Capacity

Establish research partnerships, publish findings, and develop policies for ethics and data safety.

Phase 3: Scale & Leadership

Scale proven pilots globally and host the GEMS Global AI in Education Conference.
